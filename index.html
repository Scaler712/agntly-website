FAQ

Frequently asked
questions

What exactly does Agntly do?

We build custom AI agents, automation systems, and software products for growing businesses. Our agents integrate with the tools your team already uses, live inside your communication channels like Slack or Discord, and follow your internal standard operating procedures. We handle everything from architecture to deployment and ongoing support.

How is this different from ChatGPT or other AI tools?

General-purpose AI tools answer questions. Our agents do actual work. They plug into your tools, follow your processes, coordinate across systems, and deliver measurable outcomes autonomously. Think of it as the difference between asking a chatbot a question and having a specialist team member who knows your SOPs, has access to every tool, and executes reliably every time.

How long does a typical project take?

Most MVPs ship in 2-4 weeks. Larger projects with multiple AI agents or complex integrations typically take 4-8 weeks. We build in weekly sprints with live demos so you see progress every week and can steer the direction in real time.

What does the engagement process look like?

We start by understanding your business goals, your existing tools, and the outcomes you need. From there, we scope the work, design the architecture, build and test it, deploy to production, and monitor performance. We own the full lifecycle and iterate with you until the system is delivering the results you need.

Do I own the source code?

Yes, 100%. Everything we build is yours. Full source code ownership, no vendor lock-in, no recurring licensing fees for what we build. You can take it to any other team if you choose to. We stay because we deliver, not because you're locked in.

What level of technical quality should I expect?

We hold ourselves to the highest industry standards for code quality, security, and reliability. Production-stable systems built for uptime, graceful failure handling, and consistent performance over months. Clean code, proper testing, documentation, CI/CD, and post-launch monitoring are standard on every project.
